Phase Guide

Periodization prevents plateaus and manages CNS fatigue. Choose your active block based on your recovery bandwidth.

Strength Phase

Goal: Maximize force production.
Reps: 1-5 range.
Taxation: High CNS fatigue. Requires dialed-in sleep.

Hypertrophy Phase

Goal: Muscle cross-sectional area growth.
Reps: 8-12 range.
Taxation: High metabolic fatigue, lower CNS tax.

Power & Hybrid

Goal: Force x Velocity + Base building.
Method: Moving sub-maximal weight as fast as possible.

Deload Phase

Goal: Shed accumulated fatigue.
Method: Drop volume and intensity by 40-50% for 1 week.
