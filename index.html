Phase Guide

Periodization prevents plateaus. Choose based on your current capacity and recovery bandwidth.

Max force. 1-5 reps. High CNS fatigue. Requires dialed-in sleep.

Hypertrophy

Size growth. 8-12 reps. High metabolic fatigue. Time under tension.

Force x Velocity + Base building. Move sub-maximal weight fast.
